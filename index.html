Welcome to MoviePacks!

MoviePacks is the premier destination for buying Blu-rays and DVDs in bulk. We offer bundles grouped by genre, format, director, and more.

What we do

We collect Blu-rays and DVDs from a number of different sources including bargain bins, yard sales, thriftstores, and more. We then clean the disks and cases and begin grouping them into different bundles. We're constantly looking to make new and exciting bundles with interesting themes that get you excited to continue building your collection. Because we only sell bundles, we can keep the cost per disk low and help you save some money compared to buying individual disks.
